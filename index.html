What We Believe

Heritage isn't a relic.

The Studio

Yellow House
Productions

Sri Lanka's first culture-tech XR studio, founded in 2021. We sit at the intersection of verified cultural heritage and immersive experience design - building IP that represents Sri Lanka to the world, on the world's terms.

Helixra™ is our consumer gaming franchise. Our broader work includes Project Archivist - an XR platform selected by WIPO IPMC and endorsed by Sri Lanka's National Innovation Agency - and Linseed, our licensing infrastructure for the Global South.
