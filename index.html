§04 - The Mission

Helixra™ exists because a game still being played is a culture still alive.

A playable archive of Sri Lanka's traditional games, safeguarded for humanity through verified research, community consultation, and responsible technology.

"Safeguarding requires the participation of communities, groups and, in some cases, individuals who create, maintain and transmit such heritage."

- UNESCO, 2003 Convention on Intangible Cultural Heritage

Built within the WIPO framework for Traditional Cultural Expressions: documented, attributed, non-extractive.

§10 - Methodology

How We Work

Pillar 01
Research

Primary-source documentation - temple records, colonial archives, oral histories - cross-checked with academic specialists before a single line of game logic is written.

Pillar 02
Consultation

Communities of practice review rules, naming, and representation before any chapter ships. Their authority supersedes ours, always.

Pillar 03
Technology

AI-assisted documentation under specialist oversight, trained on authenticated historical records. Non-extractive development throughout.

What We Believe

A workshop, not a vitrine.

"A tradition kept under glass is a tradition slowly losing temperature. The most reliable way to safeguard a living practice is to keep it in motion - taught, played, contested, refined. Helixra™ is a workshop, not a vitrine."

Partner With Us

Institutional collaboration, research partnerships, and grant-funded archival work.

Yellow House Productions is actively seeking institutional partners to expand the Helixra™ archive. We work within established frameworks - WIPO TCE, UNESCO 2003, and national heritage bodies - and welcome collaboration from:

  • Universities and cultural heritage research institutions
  • Government arts councils and national heritage bodies
  • International cultural funds and grant organisations (UNESCO, British Council, EU Creative Europe)
  • Museums, libraries, and archival institutions
  • Game studios interested in culturally-grounded digital experiences
  • Documentary and media production partners

We offer verified documentation, community-endorsed source material, and a non-extractive methodology that meets the standards required by international heritage frameworks.
